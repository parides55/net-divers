Cyprus
Underwater

Cyprus is a scuba diver's paradise, offering crystal-clear waters, diverse marine life, and some of the best diving conditions in the Mediterranean. With its warm climate and impressive underwater visibility, the island is ideal for both beginners and advanced divers. From vibrant reefs and fascinating sea caves to historic shipwrecks like the world-famous Zenobia, Cyprus provides unforgettable diving experiences year-round. Whether you're seeking adventure, exploration, or tranquility beneath the waves, Cyprus is the perfect destination to dive in and discover.
