Net Divers

Founded in 1994, our diving school in Cyprus has been introducing people to the wonders of the sea for nearly four decades. As a proud NAUI (National Association of Underwater Instructors) school, we follow the internationally recognized NAUI standards and manuals, ensuring the highest levels of safety, professionalism, and quality in every course we teach. From beginner dives to advanced certifications, as well as first aid training and marine awareness seminars, we’ve had the privilege of training thousands of divers who now share our love for the underwater world.

Our team of four highly trained NAUI instructors brings not only expertise but also a deep passion for the sea. We organize weekly diving trips across Cyprus, exploring shipwrecks, reefs, and hidden caves, while making sure every diver feels safe, supported, and inspired. More than just training, our goal is to create memorable experiences and lasting connections with the ocean—always with great care for marine life and the environment.

At the heart of our school is a simple belief: diving should be safe, enjoyable, and enriching. Guided by NAUI’s trusted standards and our own love for the sea, we are here to help you take your first breath underwater, refine your skills, or embark on thrilling new adventures—all while protecting the incredible marine world that makes diving so extraordinary.
